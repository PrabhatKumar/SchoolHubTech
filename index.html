  • SchoolHub is a portal founded with a simple objective - to empower students, parents and guardians to access an online academic bookstore at their doorstep.
  • At SchoolHub we are building a suite of integrated collection of academic books which are referred across multiple schools.
  • Our portal tool make it easy for students/parents/guardians to place an order of academic books package online, verify books list beforehand and we then get it hassle free deliver to your residing place.
  • We stock nearly every academic books package and most in stock orders are shipped within 24 hours (if not faster!).
